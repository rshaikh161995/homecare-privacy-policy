Home Care - Privacy Policy

Effective: November 2025
1. Introduction

Welcome to Home Care — a trusted service by Inamdar Hospital. We prioritize your privacy and are committed to transparent, secure handling of your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect essential details like your name, phone number, email address, and live location to deliver safe, efficient, and personalized healthcare services.

3. Location Access

Your location helps us pinpoint your position during home visits, streamline service booking, and power real-time SOS emergency alerts. All data is encrypted and securely transmitted to our servers.

4. Emergency (SOS) Usage

Tapping the SOS button instantly shares your real-time location and registered details with our hospital response team for rapid medical help or ambulance dispatch.

5. Camera Access

Camera permission is used only to capture photos for ID verification, patient reports, or medical documentation as needed for care delivery.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption, access controls, and secure protocols to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your data with any third parties. Your information stays strictly within Inamdar Hospital for healthcare and operational purposes only.
